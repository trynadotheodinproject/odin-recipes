Green Chile Chicken Enchilada Casserole

GO BACK TO HOME




INGREDIENTS:

  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • garlic salt to taste
  • 18 (6 inch) corn tortillas, torn in half
  • 1 (28 ounce) can green chile enchilada sauce
  • 1 (16 ounce) package shredded Monterey Jack cheese
  • 1 (8 ounce) container reduced fat sour cream

Now It's time to put it all together!


DIRECTIONS:

    Step 1

  • Preheat the oven to 370 degrees F(188 degrees C) and lightly grease the clean baking dish

    • Step 2

  • Season the chicken with garlic salt! Bake in oven until the meat is no longer pink and juices are running clear. This should take around 45 min. (insert a thermometer in center of chicken to read at least 165 degrees F or 74 degrees C)

    • Step 3

  • Let it cool down, Shred the chicken and set it aside for later. We have big plans for this ingredient!

    • Step 4

  • Hold each tortilla over an open flame from you gas stove using metal tongs for safety. Get a decent char all over for the best crunch!

    • Step 5

  • pur about a half inch of enchilada sauce onto the bottom of a baking dish. Assort the totillas into a single layer on top.

    • Step 6

  • Sprinkle with 1/2 of the chicken, 1/3 of the cheese, 1/2 of the sour cream, and 1/3 of the remaining enchilada sauce.

    • Step 7

  • Repeat layers. Coat remaining tortillas thoroughly with some of the remaining enchilada sauce and arrange on top. Sprinkle with remaining cheese and top with any remaining enchilada sauce.

    • Step 8

  • Cover and bake in the preheated oven for 45 minutes. Cool slightly before serving.
